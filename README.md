# 🚀 LaneShift — AI Creator Intelligence Platform

> Know your lane. Shift smarter.

LaneShift is an AI-powered creator intelligence platform that helps creators understand their content positioning, identify their niche, and discover **platform-specific growth strategies**.

Instead of giving generic advice, LaneShift simulates how different platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Twitch, X) reward content — and shows creators how their strategy should change depending on where they publish.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🧭 Content Positioning Analysis
- Detects your primary content lane (Gaming, Tech, Lifestyle, etc.)
- Identifies audience segments and overlap
- Calculates trust score, clarity signal, and growth potential

### 🔁 Strategic Pivot Recommendations
- Suggests adjacent niches for growth
- Explains *why* a pivot works
- Includes difficulty, risk level, and execution direction

### 💡 AI Content Idea Generator
- Generates tailored content ideas based on your niche
- Adapts ideas to platform behavior
- Supports multiple formats (short-form, tutorials, story-based, etc.)

### 📊 Performance Prediction
- Estimates engagement strength by content type
- Helps creators prioritize formats that perform best

### 🌐 Platform-Aware Intelligence (Core Feature)
- Adapts strategy based on platform selection:
  - YouTube → retention, search, structure
  - TikTok → hooks, speed, viral loops
  - Instagram → branding, aesthetics
  - Twitch → community, consistency
  - X → timing, commentary, speed

### 🔥 Platform Switching Preview (WOW Feature)
- Compare how your strategy changes across platforms
- See differences in:
  - Growth potential
  - Algorithm behavior
  - Risk factors
  - Best content formats
- Designed to feel like a real decision-making tool, not just analysis

## 🎯 Problem It Solves

Most creators struggle with:
- Not knowing what niche they’re actually in
- Posting content without clear direction
- Using the same strategy across different platforms

LaneShift solves this by turning a simple content description into:
- Clear positioning
- Platform-specific strategy
- Actionable next steps

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. User selects a platform (YouTube, TikTok, etc.)
2. Inputs a short description of their content
3. LaneShift analyzes:
   - Keywords
   - Intent
   - Audience signals
4. Generates:
   - Content lane
   - Audience breakdown
   - Pivot strategy
   - Content ideas
   - Performance predictions
5. Optionally previews how strategy changes on other platforms
